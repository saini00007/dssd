Program Overview

This Advance Diploma in Cyber Defence will help you develop expertise in defensive cybersecurity, application security, malware analysis, ethical hacking, and much more. In partnership with Symbiosis Skills and Professional University, this program provides you with a deep understanding needed to spot the opportunities for disruption in the cybersecurity industry.

  • Ethical Hacking

  • Threat Detection
