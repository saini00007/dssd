Shashank Shekhar Garuryar

Chairman

Shashank is a B.Tech ECE from BIT Sindri with MBA Marketing from IGNOU. He has domain experience of Process Automation, Safe & Smart Cities and Critical Infrastructure and has served Power, Oil & Gas, Steel, Defense, Telecom sectors in Project Management, Business Development and Business Operations for close to four decades. He brings insight into the complex Industry sector; understands the issues industries face and has a fantastic contact network to navigate complex stakeholder environments.

Shashank started his career from teaching, moved to PSU/Pvt/MNC and served Fertilizer, Hindalco, ABB, Datamatics, G4S, HFCL. He is one of the founder Members of ISA Delhi Section.

Shashank is a motivational speaker, an influencer and a relationship management cum behavioral sciences trainer. As a Coach and facilitator, he enables others to grow in their journey of self-discovery and evolution – as a person and as a professional.

He believes “Training is one of the core pillars of our success”.

Balaji Venketeshwar
