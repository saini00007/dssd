About Cybervidyapeeth

About Us

  • Cyber Vidyapeeth Foundation or CyberVersity has been up with focus on Education, Research and Training on Advanced Cyber Defense.
  • CyberVersity is committed to creating a million Cyber Rakshak over next five years.
  • CyberVersity team consist of Cyber Engineering / Defense Professionals from domains covering national defense, education, cyber defense & Cyber Offense Capability.
  • CyberVersity is all set to serve Bharat and exhorts all Cyber Professionals, Academia and organizations to join its mission of Cyber Resilient Bharat.
  • Do join us to see our common dream of “Cyber Resilient Bharat with Deep Cyber Offensive Capabilities.”

Mission

The mission of the Cyber Vidyapeeth is to Provide Education, Training & Research in the areas of Advance Cyber Defense. We shall create significantly large talent pool in Cyber Defense for Cyber Resilient Bharat.

Vision

With the mission stated above, the Foundation has envisioned to

  • Become the leading Education, Training and Research Organization in the areas of Cyber Defence & Related Technologies Including Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, 5G, IoT, Block Chain, Quantum Computing
  • Support universities, institutes, colleges, education boards and similar entities to architect, improve & enhance the Cyber Defense curricula and Train the trainer for easy adoption of new syllabus
  • Promote discourse & discussion on Cyber Defense & Related Technologies to ignite idea creation among the academia, students and cyber professionals
  • Mentor budding talent to infuse academic & technological excellence for creating next-generation Cyber Rakshak for Bharat
