Essay Course for UPSC CSE

by Vajiram & Ravi

The Essay module by Vajiram and Ravi is a one-stop solution for obtaining the skills and content needed to ace the paper. Its goal is to teach aspirants how to identify and interpret essay topics, how to write persuasively and coherently, and to offer them with Essay-relevant knowledge that serves as a framework for preparing for the Essay paper's requirements.

Commencement Date -

  • Mode – Online / Offline
  • Faculty – Brijendra Singh
  • Fees – 9000 (Exclusive of GST)
  • Duration – 2 Months

Salient Features:

  • Content lectures on a variety of essay themes, including reflective essays, gender- related concerns, contemporary international relations, and improving writing skills, among others.
  • Mini assignments during the class, to reinforce the concepts.
  • Detailed handouts for each class that contain comprehensive content (theory, data, examples, quotations, and so on) to serve as a repository of information.
  • 3 model tests on the UPSC Mains Essay Paper format, followed by discussions.
  • Detailed evaluation of answer scripts using standardized criteria to accurately identify areas for improvement.
